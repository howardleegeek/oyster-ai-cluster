Three Pillars 三大支柱

Sovereignty by Design 主权即设计

Pillar A 支柱 A

Physical Sovereignty 物理主权

Hardware is territory. Each ClawPhone is a border node. You own the chip, not a subscription. 硬件即领土。每台 ClawPhone 都是一个边界节点。你拥有的是芯片，不是订阅。

Pillar B 支柱 B

Algorithmic Sovereignty 算法主权

Algorithms are law. NanoClaw executes locally; intelligence is distributed, not centralized. 算法即法律。NanoClaw 本地执行；智能是分布式的，不是中心化的。

Pillar C 支柱 C

Economic Sovereignty 经济主权

Labor becomes value. Idle time converts to compute contributions that benefit the operator. 劳动变成价值。空闲时间转化为算力贡献，收益归运营者所有。
