# GTM: Article Pipeline

Phase 1: Build trend data layer with 3 adapters (Google Trends, Reddit, TwitFast). Phase 2: Article generation engine with quality gate and revision loop. Phase 3: Distribution layer (X Articles publisher via CDP + tweet thread posting). Launch: 3 articles per day, auto-scheduled at 9am/1pm/6pm Beijing time. Measure: engagement rate, follower growth, article read time.
