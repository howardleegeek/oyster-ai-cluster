## 页面: /content/new (新建) 和 /content (列表)

## API对接
- POST /api/v1/content/generate → 调用Writer
- GET /api/v1/content/:id → 获取状态
- POST /api/v1/content/:id/review → Reviewer审核
- POST /api/v1/publish/twitter → 发布
